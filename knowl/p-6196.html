Two notes about how Exercise 5.6.6.1 is coded: (i) as explained in the problem header, you need responses to every single entry before you can get individual parts marked as correct; if you enter only an answer for (a) and submit, (a) will be marked wrong regardless. And (ii), in this problem, the notation "SIMP(2)" is actually what we have called "SIMP(4)" in our previous work. Different authors use different notation, and the author of this WeBWorK exercise chooses to write "SIMP(n)" where we have written "SIMP(2n)" in Section 5.6.
