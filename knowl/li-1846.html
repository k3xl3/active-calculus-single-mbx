Item 2.6.6.9.d.

By the chain rule,

\begin{equation*} g'(v) = \frac{1}{ \frac{\arctan(v)}{\arcsin(v) + v^2} } \cdot \frac{d}{dv}\left[ \frac{\arctan(v)}{\arcsin(v) + v^2} \right] \end{equation*}

Thus, by the quotient rule,

\begin{equation*} g'(v) = \frac{1}{\frac{\arctan(v)}{\arcsin(v) + v^2}} \cdot \frac{(\arcsin(v) + v^2) \cdot \frac{1}{1+v^2} - \arctan(v) \cdot \left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-v^2}} + 2v \right)}{(\arcsin(v) + v^2)^2} \end{equation*}
