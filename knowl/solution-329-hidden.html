\(\int\,u\,v'\,dx = u\cdot v - \int u'\,v\,dx\)) with \(u = z+1\) and \(v' = e^{5 z}\text{,}\) so that \(u' = 1\) and \(v = {1\over 5} e^{5 z}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \int\,\left(z+1\right)e^{5z}\,dz = \frac{z+1}{5}\,e^{5 z} - \int\,{1\over 5} e^{5 z}\,dz = \frac{z+1}{5}\,e^{5 z} - {1\over 25} e^{5 z} + C. \end{equation*}
