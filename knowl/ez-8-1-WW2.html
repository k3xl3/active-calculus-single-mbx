Exercise 1. Limits of five sequences.

Match the formulas with the descriptions of the behavior of the sequence as \(n\to\infty\text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle s_n = 1 + \cos(n)/n\)

  2. \(\displaystyle s_n = n(n+1) - 1\)

  3. \(\displaystyle s_n = (n+1)/n\)

  4. \(\displaystyle s_n = (\sin(n)/n)\)

  5. \(\displaystyle s_n = n\sin(n)/(n+1)\)

  1. converges to zero through positive and negative numbers

  2. does not converge, but doesn't go to \(\pm\infty\)

  3. diverges to \(\infty\)

  4. converges to one from above and below

  5. converges to one from above
