\(2 x^{3/2}\) in disguise. So the arc length is
\begin{equation*} \int_{2}^{5}\,\sqrt{1 + (f'(x))^2}\,dx = \int_{2}^{5}\,\sqrt{1 + (3 x^{1/2})^2}\,dx = \int_{2}^{5}\,\sqrt{1 + 9 x}\,dx. \end{equation*}
With \(w = 1 + 9 x\text{,}\) this becomes
\begin{equation*} \int_{2}^{5}\,\sqrt{1 + 9 x}\,dx = {{1\over 9}} \int_{19}^{46}\,\sqrt{w}\,dw = {{2\over 27}} ( 46^{3/2} - 19^{3/2} ). \end{equation*}
